About Me

I am currently an undergraduate student at Ocean University of China (OUC) , majoring in Electronic Information Engineering. I have been admitted to the Master's program in Computer Science at the School of Computer Science and Technology, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS), starting in September 2026.

During my undergraduate study, I engaged in several research projects, co-supervised by Assistant Professor Shishi Qiao and Professor Haiyong Zheng from the College of Information Science and Engineering, Ocean University of China. My research at this stage centered on hashing learning and image retrieval, encompassing areas such as fine-grained image retrieval, underwater image retrieval, and self-supervised hashing learning.

Now I am pursuing my postgraduate study at VIPL of Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Sciences, University of Chinese Academy of Sciences, research focus on end-to-end autonomous driving, including world model, multi-modal fusion and etc.
